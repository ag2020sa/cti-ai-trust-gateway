# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes demonstrating empathy, respecting differing viewpoints, giving and
accepting constructive feedback, taking responsibility for mistakes, and focusing on what is best
for the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized language or imagery,
trolling, insults, personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and other
conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may remove,
edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are inconsistent with
this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code applies in project spaces and when an individual officially represents the project in
public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `ag2020.sa@gmail.com`. This address is authorized only for
conduct reports; do not send security vulnerabilities, credentials, private CTI, or other sensitive
project data to it. Do not disclose personal or sensitive information in a public issue.

Maintainers will follow the Contributor Covenant Community Impact Guidelines when determining
corrections, warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
